HTML & CSS

I volunteered in a study as a requirement for my psychology lecture in university about three years ago. The study involved us learning how to code in javascript for beginners and we filled out a survey at the end about our experience. Overall, I thought it felt natural and I was surprised how easy it was to code. Last year I started to pick up coding again as I became interested in how hacking works and how there were ethical hackers as well. I did HTML and CSS for little bit. Then I tried learning some Python 3 and practing for and while loops. Right now I am taking a web development course on Udemy by Angela Yu. I love the course and have learned a lot of useful information. I am comfortable with HTML and CSS so far and love being creative in the front-end development. I will continue learning other coding languages in the back-end of development very soon.

Tennis

Tennis is forever in my heart and soul. I started playing very young when I could barely lift a racket up off the ground. I played in many USTA tournaments and the school teams. In high school, I was on the varsity team all four years in all positions. I didn't get to play in college and I wish I did. I feel so lucky now that I get to be a part of my former high school tennis team as an assistant coach. I get to play tennis and help coach my team during matches. I do not know what my life would be like without tennis. I honestly cannot live without it.
